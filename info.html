History
Jihan Motors was founded in 1997 and is part of the Jihan Group, which has a strong presence throughout the country with operations in banking, insurance, education, construction, real estate, transportation, logistics, food and beverages, heavy machinery, and petroleum. It was the first private company to import Toyota cars from Japan through Tomine Company in 2004, importing approximately 70,000 Toyota cars to Iraq. It is the first and leading provider of the Toyota Beige Taxi project and the Farmers' Union project in Kurdistan, successfully implementing both projects. It owns a network of 9 facilities affiliated with Toyota in Iraq, 4 of which are authorized Toyota dealerships (3S facilities - Sales, Service & Spare Parts), from Toyota Motors, Japan.
